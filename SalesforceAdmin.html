1. Organization Setup

  • Fiscal Year Setup

    • Setup >> Quick Find >> Company Settings >> Fiscal Year

    Sr. No Description Salesforce Help Link
    1.0 If your fiscal year follows the Gregorian calendar, but does not start in January, you can define a standard fiscal year with a different starting month Link
    2.0 Standard fiscal years follow the Gregorian calendar, but can start on the first day of any month of the year
    3.0 After you enable custom fiscal years, you canâ€™t disable the feature
    4.0 After enabling custom fiscal years, your organization's fiscal years will no longer be automatically defined by salesforce.com. The only fiscal years available for reports, quotas, forecasts, and so on will be those defined by your organization
    5.0 If you have forecasting features enabled, creating the initial custom fiscal year deletes all quotas and adjustments from that year forward.

  • Organization's Business Hours Setup

    • Setup >> Quick Find >> Company Settings >> Business Hours

    Sr. No Description Salesforce Help Link
    1.0 Setting Business Hours specify the hours when your support team is available to serve customers Link
    2.0 Setting business hours lets you apply specific time zones and locations: Milestones in entitlement processes, Entitlement processes,Cases,Case escalation rules
    3.0 Leave the business hours start and end times blank and the 24 hours checkbox deselected to indicate that the support team is not available at all that day.

  • Currency Management

    Enable Multiple Currencies

    Sr. No Description Salesforce Help Link
    1.0 Before your organization can use multiple currencies, the feature must be turned on Link
    2.0 Enabling multiple currencies introduces permanent changes in your organization
    3.0 Ensure that your selected currency locale is the default (base or corporate) currency that you want to use for current and future records and exchange rate is based on that corporate currency

    Currency Setup

    Setup >> Quick Find >> Company Settings >> Company Information >> Currency Setup

    Sr. No Description Salesforce Help Link
    1.0 Used to define all the currencies used by your organization Link
    2.0 Used to Enable / Disable advance currency Management (dated exchange rate)
    3.0 Used to Enable / Disable Parenthetical Currency Conversion. This is enabled automatically once advance Currency is enabled. Disabling it will display the currency in select currency value only
